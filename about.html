About Deshmed

A leading pharmaceutical company driven by niche marketing.

Deshmed Healthcare is one of the leading pharmaceutical companies, playing a pivotal role in introducing the concept of 'niche marketing' in India.

Therapeutic Focus

Leaders across diverse therapeutic segments.

Today, Deshmed ranks among the leaders in therapeutic segments such as orthopedics, rheumatology, ENT, neurology, women's healthcare (WHC), and gastrointestinal (GI) care.

The company also maintains a strong presence in segments like pediatrics, pain management, and dermatology.

Our vision

A leading pharmaceutical company.

Our vision is to be a leading pharmaceutical company by providing high quality, affordable and therapeutic solutions for patients with diverse medical needs.
